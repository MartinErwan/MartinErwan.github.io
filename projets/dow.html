Context

Dow's engineering team in Tarragona needed a way to predict how changes in plastic film chemical composition would affect their mechanical properties — a process that typically requires costly and time-consuming lab experiments.

Starting from experimental data stored across internal databases, I built a full pipeline from data extraction to a deployed prediction app used directly by engineers to simulate formulation outcomes in seconds.
