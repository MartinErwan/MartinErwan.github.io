Context

TotalEnergies operates subsea fiber-optic infrastructure that can be repurposed as a passive acoustic sensor network (DAS — Distributed Acoustic Sensing). The goal was to design an AI system capable of detecting marine species from these raw vibration signals, without labeled ground truth data.

The project covered the full pipeline: building an acoustic species database from scientific literature, processing raw signals on an HPC cluster, training deep learning models for unsupervised feature extraction, and localizing species spatio-temporally from their acoustic signatures.
