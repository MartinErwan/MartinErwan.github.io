Context

Macro-steatosis (liver fat accumulation) is a critical factor in transplant surgery. This project, part of the BOPA innovation chair (AP-HP), aimed to automate its estimation intraoperatively using near-infrared spectrometry — replacing subjective visual assessment by surgeons.

My role covered finalizing the Android app, setting up the cloud infrastructure, and developing the ML pipeline for model training, tracking and comparison. The app was shipped to the surgical team at Hôpital Paul Brousse for real patient data collection.
